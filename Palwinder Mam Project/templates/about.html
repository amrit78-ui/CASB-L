About the Project

Understanding CASB-IRL

A next-generation AI framework that decodes student behavior, predicts learning trajectories, and empowers educators with explainable, actionable insights - powered by Inverse Reinforcement Learning.

Framework Overview

What is CASB-IRL?

CASB-IRL is an advanced educational AI framework that combines Inverse Reinforcement Learning with context-aware feature engineering to model, analyze, and predict a student's learning behavior with remarkable accuracy.

By observing student behaviors - study habits, attendance, stress levels, social engagement, and more - the system infers the underlying reward structure that drives those behaviors and predicts future outcomes.

C
Context-Aware Considers environmental, emotional, and demographic context for each student individually.
A
Adaptive Dynamically adjusts predictions based on changing behavioral patterns over time.
S
Student Behavior Focuses on multi-dimensional behavioral signals, not just academic scores.
B · IRL
Inverse Reinforcement Learning Infers reward functions from behavior to understand what motivates each student.
